Raewyn Duvall Biography

My name is Raewyn Arcadia Duvall. I was born in Chattanooga, TN, on June 14, 1994 to Mary and Dan Duvall. I went to an all Girls High School and now attend Tufts University.

I have 3 siblings and a dog. My oldest siter, the other engineer of the family, is 33 with a loving husband and 5 rambunctious children. Both she and her husband graduated from MIT. My second sister is 31 and working for the state department after attending Yale, University of Tokyo, and Fletcher. My brother is 26 and attending Cornell University. My dog is half boarder collie half huskey and is named Potasium Nine, K-9, and goes by Kassy.

I love theater (stage managing specifically), building things, programming, fencing, taekwondo, swimming, and sleeping. Especially sleeping. My biggest accomplisment was stage managing a full production of Les Mis at my high school with only 10 techs to help out.

I have travelled the world, my favorite places being New Zealand and France so far. I hope to travel more as I get older.
